p5.js Device Sampler

An interactive showcase demonstrating how p5.js can interface with various hardware inputs: microphone, camera, audio synthesis, and user peripherals.

01 / Audio Input

Microphone Reactive

This sketch uses p5.AudioIn() to capture audio from your device's microphone. The amplitude (volume) of the incoming audio is mapped to the size and color of a central shape.

Note: You must click inside the frame to start the audio context, and allow browser microphone permissions.

02 / Video Input

Webcam Capture

Using createCapture(VIDEO), this sketch accesses your device's camera. The video feed is drawn onto the canvas and flipped horizontally to act like a mirror.

Note: Requires granting camera permissions in your browser.

03 / Audio Output

Sound Synthesizer

Generates sound using a p5.Oscillator. Click and drag across the canvas: the X-axis controls frequency (pitch), and the Y-axis controls amplitude (volume).

04 / Peripherals

Mouse & Keyboard

A simple drawing application demonstrating event handling. It tracks mouseX and mouseY for drawing, and listens for keyPressed() to change brush colors and sizes.

Built with p5.js
